## What I work on

I currently work on V2V autonomous driving perception: turning multi-vehicle LiDAR into BEV representations, reconstructing occluded regions, and building reproducible training pipelines that are worth trusting.

That robotics line is backed by a second strength: evaluation-minded AI systems. My SmartEarth internship pushed me into retrieval benchmarking, which later became `rag-eval-pipeline` and now informs how I build application-layer systems like `coval`.

I also like shipping practical software outside research, from full-stack backend-heavy products to consumer-facing apps and small multiplayer systems.

## Trajectory

- `Jun 2024-Aug 2024` - SmartEarth internship: evaluated geo-domain RAG retrieval setups and documented real retrieval failure modes.
